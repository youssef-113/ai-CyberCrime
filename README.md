# 🛡️ AI Cybercrime Evidence Builder  
### Smart Legal Evidence Structuring System (Egyptian Law)

---

## 📌 Project Overview

**AI Cybercrime Evidence Builder** is an AI-powered legal-tech system designed to help victims of online crimes in Egypt automatically structure, verify, and prepare legally supported complaint reports.

Instead of a simple legal chatbot, this system allows users to upload digital evidence (chat screenshots, PDFs, images). It extracts key information using AI, organizes everything into a structured timeline, links it to relevant Egyptian legal articles, and generates a ready-to-submit official complaint report.

Our mission is to make digital justice:

- Structured  
- Evidence-backed  
- Legally grounded  
- Accessible to everyone  

---

## 🎯 Problem We Solve

Victims of cybercrimes (blackmail, scams, threats, defamation) often face:

- Difficulty organizing scattered digital evidence  
- Lack of legal knowledge  
- Fear of submitting incomplete complaints  
- Weak documentation leading to rejected cases  
- Time-consuming manual report preparation  

There is currently no automated system that transforms raw screenshots into a structured, legally supported complaint report.

---

## 💡 Our Solution

Our system performs the following steps:

1. 📂 Accepts uploaded evidence (images, screenshots, PDFs)
2. 🔎 Uses OCR to extract:
   - Dates  
   - Phone numbers  
   - Names  
   - Bank accounts  
   - Financial amounts  
3. 🗂 Automatically builds a chronological timeline
4. 🤖 Uses LLM classification to identify case type:
   - Blackmail  
   - Scam  
   - Threat  
   - Defamation  
5. 📚 Uses RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) to retrieve relevant Egyptian legal articles
6. ⚖ Applies a multi-agent verification layer to validate claims
7. 📊 Calculates an **Evidence Strength Score**
8. 📄 Generates an official complaint-ready PDF including:
   - Case summary  
   - Timeline  
   - Evidence list  
   - Legal references  
   - Confidence score  
   - Missing evidence suggestions

## 🧠 System Architecture

- **Backend:** FastAPI

- **Containerization:** Docker & Docker Compose  
- **OCR Engine:** Text extraction from images & PDFs  
- **LLM Classification Layer**  
- **RAG Legal Retrieval System**  
- **Multi-Agent Verification System**  
- **Evidence Strength Scoring Engine**
